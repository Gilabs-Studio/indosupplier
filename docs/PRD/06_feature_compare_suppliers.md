# Feature: Compare Suppliers

## Purpose

Buyers — especially foreign buyers or procurement teams making high-value decisions — often want to evaluate multiple suppliers against the same criteria before deciding who to contact. The Compare Suppliers feature provides a side-by-side structured view of up to 4 suppliers, so buyers can make informed choices without opening multiple tabs or asking the same questions repeatedly.

---

## Who Uses This

- **Buyer** — the only role that can use this feature
- Most useful for: procurement managers, foreign buyers doing due diligence, teams that need to present options internally before deciding

---

## How to Start a Comparison

There are three ways to add suppliers to the comparison:

1. **From search results**: each supplier card has a checkbox; select 2–4 suppliers, then click the "Compare (N)" button that appears in the bottom action bar
2. **From a supplier's profile page**: click "Add to Compare" button — the supplier is added to the current comparison selection
3. **From the Bookmarks list**: select saved suppliers and click "Compare Selected"

When a buyer has 1 supplier selected, they are prompted to add at least one more. When they have 4, the "Add to Compare" button on other profiles is disabled until they remove one.

---

## The Comparison Page

The comparison page is a full-width table where:
- Each column represents one supplier
- Each row represents one data point
- Missing data (fields the supplier has not filled in) is shown as "–" so buyers know it is not available, not hidden

### Data Rows Shown

**Identity**
- Company name and logo
- Company type (PT / CV / UD / etc.)
- City and province
- Year established
- Number of employees (range)

**Verification & Trust**
- Verification level badge (Registered / Verified / Premium Verified)
- Tax status: PKP or Non-PKP
- Tax Verified badge (if documents confirmed)
- Certifications held (shown as badge icons: ISO, Halal, BPOM, SNI, etc.)

**Performance**
- Response rate (percentage)
- Average response time (e.g. "Usually within 6 hours")
- Total RFQs responded to (lifetime)
- Star rating (average)
- Number of reviews

**Capacity**
- Production capacity
- Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)
- Starting price range for main products (if supplier has filled this in)

**Export**
- Export experience: Yes / No
- Countries exported to
- Export documents held (COO, Fumigation Certificate, etc.)

**Contact & Communication**
- WhatsApp: available / not listed
- Email: available / not listed
- Website: link or "–"
- Business hours
- Languages spoken

---

## Actions from the Comparison Page

- **Send RFQ to all**: sends a Multi-Supplier RFQ to all suppliers currently in the comparison (subject to buyer's score limit of 5 suppliers per RFQ)
- **Send RFQ to this supplier**: send a Specific RFQ to one supplier in the comparison
- **Remove from comparison**: remove one column and optionally add another supplier
- **Export as PDF**: download the comparison table as a formatted PDF — useful for presenting to a manager or team before making a sourcing decision
- **Share comparison link**: generates a unique URL for this specific comparison that can be shared with a colleague (they do not need to be logged in to view the comparison, but must log in to send RFQs)

---

## Limits

- Minimum 2 suppliers to open the comparison view
- Maximum 4 suppliers at one time
- Comparison state is saved in the session — if the buyer closes the browser, they need to rebuild the comparison (no permanent saved comparisons)
- The shared link is valid for 7 days

---

## Notes

- Suppliers are never notified that they are being compared
- The comparison feature does not affect any supplier's ranking or statistics
- Buyers can compare suppliers across different categories (e.g. comparing a textile supplier with a packaging supplier) — the rows still show the same data points; some will simply show "–" for fields not applicable
