# Feature: Buyer Account

## Purpose

The buyer account gives buyers a personalized experience on the platform — saving their search history, managing their RFQs, and building their Lead Quality Score over time. The buyer interface is completely separate from the supplier interface; buyers never see supplier-side menus or dashboards.

---

## Who Uses This

- **Any person or company** looking to source products from Indonesian suppliers
- Local buyers: procurement managers, SME owners, startup sourcing teams
- Foreign buyers: international importers, multinational companies, trade visitors

---

## Registration Flow

1. Go to the registration page → select "I am a Buyer"
2. Enter: full name, company name (optional), country, company email, WhatsApp number
3. Verify email via activation link
4. Complete a short profile: industry, type of products frequently searched, purchase frequency
5. Account is immediately active — buyer can start searching

**Optional step to increase Lead Quality Score:**
- Upload company document (SIUP, NIB, company registration) for admin review
- This raises the score and unlocks Broadcast RFQ access

---

## Buyer Homepage

The buyer homepage is different from a supplier's homepage. It shows:
- Recommended suppliers based on search history and favorite categories
- Featured suppliers (those with Featured Slot ads)
- Popular categories
- Active Broadcast RFQs from other buyers (publicly visible board — without buyer identity revealed)
- Newly joined suppliers this week

---

## Buyer Navigation

The buyer interface has its own navigation with no supplier-side menus:

- **Search Suppliers** — main search with AI Search toggle
- **Categories** — browse by industry category
- **My RFQs** — inbox of all RFQs sent and responses received
- **Compare** — the supplier comparison tool
- **Bookmarks** — saved/shortlisted suppliers
- **Notifications** — new supplier in a saved category, RFQ replies
- **Profile** — buyer's own account settings and Lead Quality Score status

---

## Buyer Features

### Search & Discovery
- Full-text search and multi-dimensional filters (see Search & Discovery feature file)
- AI Search: type a need in natural language, get structured keyword suggestions
- Browse by category

### Supplier Profile Viewing
- View all public supplier information
- See trust signals: response rate, certifications, tax status, company type, reviews
- "Send RFQ" and "Add to Compare" buttons on every profile

### Bookmarking
- Save suppliers to a personal shortlist
- Organize bookmarks (optional tags or notes per saved supplier)
- Access bookmarked suppliers quickly for future RFQs or comparison

### RFQ Management
- View all RFQs sent: pending, responded, closed
- See supplier responses in a unified inbox
- Continue conversation with a specific supplier after initial RFQ
- Archive old RFQs

### Supplier Comparison
- Add up to 4 suppliers to compare
- View side-by-side comparison of key data points
- Send an RFQ to all compared suppliers at once from the comparison page

### Reviews & Ratings
- After interacting with a supplier (via RFQ), buyer can leave a rating (1–5 stars) and written review
- Buyer must be logged in to leave a review
- Each buyer can review each supplier once per interaction period

### Notifications
- RFQ responses from suppliers
- New suppliers joined in a category the buyer has bookmarked
- Broadcast RFQ status updates
- Account verification status updates

## Lead Quality Score

Every buyer account has a Lead Quality Score between 0 and 100. This score is **shown to suppliers** when they receive an RFQ — it helps suppliers decide which RFQs to prioritize.

### How the Score is Built

| Component | Points | How to Earn |
|---|---|---|
| Verified company email | 30 | Email with a company domain (not Gmail/Yahoo) |
| Verified company documents | 40 | Upload SIUP, NIB, or company registration; admin approves |
| Complete profile | 15 | Fill all profile fields: name, company, country, industry, description |
| Interaction history | 10 | Replied to suppliers who responded to past RFQs |
| Platform activity | 5 | Regular login, no spam history, written reviews |

### Score Tiers

| Score | Label | Badge | What It Means for Suppliers |
|---|---|---|---|
| 80–100 | Premium Buyer | Dark blue "Verified Buyer" | High-quality lead, worth prioritizing |
| 50–79 | Trusted Buyer | Green "Trusted" | Reasonably verified, worth responding to |
| 20–49 | New Buyer | Grey "New" | New or incomplete profile |
| 0–19 | Unverified | Yellow "Unverified" | Not yet verified — supplier may choose to skip |

### Score Behavior Rules
- The score is **never shown to the buyer directly** — they see a "profile completeness" indicator instead, with suggestions to improve it
- The score is visible to suppliers on every incoming RFQ notification
- The score affects daily RFQ limits: Unverified = 2/day, New = 5/day, Trusted = 15/day, Premium = unlimited
- Only buyers with score ≥ 50 can send Multi-Supplier RFQs (2–5 suppliers)
- Only buyers with score ≥ 80 can use Broadcast RFQ (send to all suppliers in a category)

## Buyer Account Settings

- Edit personal information (name, company, country, contact)
- Change email (requires re-verification)
- Change password
- Notification preferences (which notifications to receive via WA vs email)
- Language preference (ID or EN)
- Delete account (right to erasure — all personal data removed)
