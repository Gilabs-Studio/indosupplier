# Feature: Advertising System

## Purpose

The advertising system is the primary revenue source for IndoSupplier. It allows suppliers to pay for increased visibility on the platform — appearing in premium positions that buyers notice first. The system has three layers: **Featured Slots** (structured premium products), **Auction Placement** (competitive bidding for the top position), and **Regular Ads** (standard direct-buy slots).

All ad placements are clearly labeled so buyers always know which results are paid and which are organic.

---

## Who Uses This

- **Supplier** — purchases ad products from their dashboard
- **Admin** — opens/closes auction sessions, reviews ad content, manages pricing, monitors fill rates

---

## Layer 1 — Featured Slots (Structured Premium Products)

Four distinct premium ad products, each with a fixed position and visual treatment. Suppliers buy these directly without bidding.

---

### 🏠 Homepage Featured Banner
**The most premium and expensive ad product on the platform.**

- Appears at the very top of the platform homepage — the first thing any visitor sees
- Format: large horizontal banner with supplier logo, tagline, and a call-to-action button
- If more than one supplier buys this slot in the same period, banners rotate every 30 seconds
- Maximum 3 banners active at the same time
- Ideal for suppliers who want maximum brand awareness across all buyers, regardless of category
- Price range: highest tier — targets established suppliers with marketing budgets

---

### ⭐ Best Supplier
**A curated endorsement — only the platform's top-verified suppliers can buy this.**

- Supplier appears in a dedicated "Best Suppliers" section on the homepage and category pages
- The section rotates among active Best Suppliers every 30 seconds (up to 5 shown at a time)
- Visual: prominent card with a gold star label, clearly distinguished from regular listings
- **Eligibility requirements** (supplier must meet all):
  - Verification Level 2 or above
  - Response rate above 60%
  - At least 3 reviews with an average rating above 4.0
- If a supplier no longer meets the criteria during the slot period, the slot is paused and admin is notified
- Maximum 3 Best Supplier slots per category per 14-day period

---

### 🔥 Hot Supplier
**A "trending" signal for suppliers gaining momentum.**

- Supplier gets a flame icon and "Hot Supplier" label on their profile card throughout the platform
- Appears in a "Currently Popular" section on the homepage and category pages
- The "hot" status is based on a combination of: recent profile views, RFQs received, and a paid boost
- Suppliers pay to push their profile into the candidate pool for this label; the algorithm then selects based on actual engagement
- Maximum 5 Hot Supplier slots per category per 7-day period

---

### 🔝 Page 1 / Top Search
**Guaranteed to appear on the first page of search results for a chosen category or keyword.**

- Supplier's card appears above organic results (but below Auction Placement slots)
- Labeled "Sponsor" or "Top" clearly
- Supplier selects which category or keyword combination they want to target
- Maximum 3 slots per category page at the same time
- This is useful for suppliers who want consistent visibility without the unpredictability of an auction

---

## Layer 2 — Auction Placement

**The most prominent position on any category page — above everything else, including Featured Slots.**

### What It Is
- 2–3 large card slots at the very top of every category search results page
- Cards are visually larger than regular supplier cards (approximately 2x the height)
- Gold border and "Featured Supplier" label
- Includes: larger logo, highlighted description, verification badge, and direct "Send RFQ" button on the card
- Available positions are sold via competitive bidding

### Bidding Process

**Opening a session (done by Admin):**
1. Admin selects a category, sets the number of slots (2 or 3), slot duration (7, 14, or 30 days), minimum bid amount, and bidding period (3–7 days)
2. Session opens and is visible to all suppliers in that category

**Placing a bid (done by Supplier):**
1. Supplier goes to their dashboard → "Advertising" → "Auction Placement" tab
2. Sees all currently open auction sessions
3. Selects the category and duration they want, enters their bid amount (must be ≥ minimum bid)
4. A refundable deposit is charged at the time of bidding
5. Supplier sees their current **rank position** (e.g. "You are currently ranked 2nd") — they never see the exact bid amounts of other suppliers

**Closing the session (automatic or manual):**
1. At the end of the bidding period, the system automatically selects winners: highest bids win the available slots
2. Winners are notified via WhatsApp and email
3. Winners confirm payment within 24 hours; ad goes live on the chosen start date
4. Losing bidders receive a full refund of their deposit automatically
5. Results (who won, which slots) are published on the auction page after the session closes

### Auction Rules
- A supplier can win a maximum of 2 slots per category in the same period
- A bid can be withdrawn up until 12 hours before the auction closes
- Minimum bid is set by admin per category and reviewed periodically
- Winners who do not confirm payment within 24 hours forfeit their slot (next highest bidder is offered it)

## Layer 3 — Regular Ads (Direct Buy, No Auction)

Standard ad slots available for direct purchase — first come, first served based on slot availability.

| Ad Product | Position | Format |
|---|---|---|
| Sponsored Listing | Within search results, above organic results | Standard supplier card with "Sponsored" badge |
| Category Banner | Top of a category page, below Auction Placement | Horizontal banner image |
| Homepage Banner | Homepage, below the hero section | Medium-size banner card |
| Sidebar Ad | Sidebar of a supplier's profile page | Small banner (visible to buyers browsing a competitor's profile) |

### Purchase Flow
1. Supplier opens dashboard → "Advertising" → "Regular Ads" tab
2. Selects the ad product type and target category/page
3. Sees a calendar showing which slots are already taken and which are available
4. Selects start date and duration (7, 14, or 30 days)
5. Uploads ad content: title, short description, image (must match size specification)
6. Previews how the ad will look before submitting
7. Pays → ad goes into admin review queue
8. Admin approves or requests changes within 1 business day
9. Ad goes live automatically on the chosen start date after approval

---

## Ad Content Review

All ad content (both Featured Slots and Regular Ads) is reviewed by Admin before going live.

Admin checks for:
- No misleading claims (e.g. false certifications, fake pricing)
- No prohibited content
- Correct image dimensions and resolution
- Text within character limits

Admin can:
- Approve (ad goes live)
- Request revision (supplier is notified with comments, asked to resubmit)
- Reject (with reason; supplier refunded)

---

## Transparency for Buyers

- All paid ad placements are clearly labeled: "Featured Supplier," "Sponsored," "Top," "Hot Supplier," "Best Supplier"
- No paid slot can hide the fact that it is paid
- Organic search results are never mixed in under a paid label

---

## Ad Statistics (Supplier View)

For every active ad, the supplier sees:
- **Impressions**: how many times the ad was displayed
- **Clicks**: how many buyers clicked through to their profile
- **RFQ Conversions**: how many buyers sent an RFQ after clicking this ad
- **Active period**: start date, end date, days remaining
